Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a goal?

Tap the + button on the dashboard to create a new goal. You can optionally answer personalized questions to get an AI-generated plan tailored just for you.

How does the AI work?

Aclio uses advanced AI to break down your goals into actionable steps. You can also use "Expand" to get more details on any step, or "Do it for me" to get ready-to-use solutions.

How do I upgrade to Premium?

Go to Settings and tap "Upgrade to Premium" to unlock unlimited goals, AI features, and more. Premium includes a 3-day free trial!
